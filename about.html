About

A company home for digital product IP.

ENVentures LTD is a private limited company created to own, steward, and develop technology products and related intellectual property.

Operating purpose

Simple structure, long-term ownership.

ENVentures provides a clear legal and operational home for product assets, brand assets, source code, domain names, and related commercial work.

The company's first owned product is Fameally. As ownership transfers complete, ENVentures will act as the company behind the product, helping keep product development, customer communication, and legal responsibilities aligned.

This website is an informational company site. Product-specific information remains available through Fameally.
